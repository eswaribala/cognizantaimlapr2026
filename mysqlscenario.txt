🛒 End-to-End E-Commerce Scenario (Python + MySQL)
🎯 Business Context

You are building the backend for an e-commerce platform similar to Amazon or Flipkart.

The system must handle:

Product catalog
Customer orders
Inventory management
Payments (simulated)
🔹 Core Scenario
🧩 Problem Statement

A customer places an order for multiple products. Your system must:

Check if all products exist
Verify sufficient stock
Deduct stock
Create an order
Insert order items
Ensure data consistency (all succeed or fail together)
